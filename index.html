Unforgettable Moments

💻 The Windows Restoration Victory

Remember when your PC hadn't been functioning for months? We spent that day working on it together, troubleshooting, trying different solutions. When we finally got Windows restored and your computer came back to life - that feeling of success! It was such a CS student moment, and it showed how we could solve problems together.

🏛️ Igongo - Seeing Through Your Eyes

The day we went to Igongo Cultural Centre will always stay with me. I had been there almost every evening, but it was your first time. What amazed me was how you made me realize so many things I had never noticed before - details, perspectives, the beauty in things I had walked past countless times.

That's who you are, Vee. You see what others miss. You notice every little detail in the environment around you. You showed me things that were there all along, but I was blind to them. That skill, that beautiful mind of yours - it's going to take you so far in life.

That gesture wasn't just about the money - it was about you showing me what true friendship means. You've always been supportive, always been there, and I will never forget that.

🗣️ Your Communication Skills
